VARIETIES

Silver Vine Kiwi

Hot Pepper Silver Vine Kiwi is a type of kiwi fruit with a unique flavor profile. While the traditional kiwi is sour and tart, this variety is sweet and spicy.

Red Kiwi

Rainbow Red kiwis are small fruits that have an oval to ovate shape with a distinct dent on the bottom of the fruit, a distinguishing factor between this specialty variety and other kiwi cultivars.

Yellow kiwi

The yellow kiwi or golden kiwifruit is a variety of kiwifruit developed by the company Zespri International Ltd. The yellow kiwi is a different species but of the same genus as the green one.

Abbott Kiwi

The Abbott Kiwi (Actinidia Deliciosa 'Abbott') is a vigorous vine with medium sized oblong fruit. Its fruit has brownish skin, soft hairs and light green flesh.

Arctic Beauty Kiwi

Viktor Arctic Beauty Kiwi is a unique kiwi fruit native to northern Europe. This sweet and juicy fruit has golden yellow-green skin with tender red-orange flesh

Pavel male silver vine kiwi

One unique type of kiwi fruit is the Pavel Male Silver Vine Kiwi, a brown-skinned fruit distinguished by its white pulp and black seeds.
